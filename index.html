Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Lumi cost?

+

Lumi costs a fraction of hiring a full-time employee. There's a one-time setup fee based on your needs, and after that, you only pay per minute of usage. Current pricing is $0.12 per minute. Book a quick call with our team and we'll build a package that fits your business.

Can Lumi have a custom personality or script?

+

Absolutely. Think of Lumi as a trainable employee. You can customize how she speaks, what she says, and how she responds to different situations. It's like hiring someone—only faster, more consistent, and always available.

How long does it take to integrate Lumi into my business?

+

It depends on your needs, but most businesses are up and running within a few days to a couple of weeks. We'll walk you through the whole process to make it smooth and fast.

What happens if Lumi can't answer a customer's question?

+

Lumi is trained to manage almost every situation. But in the rare case it can't, it will instantly transfer the call to your team, so your customer never hits a dead end. Simple as that.

What if Lumi doesn't work for my business?

+

We offer a 14-day guarantee. If you feel Lumi hasn't made a positive impact, you can cancel and request a full refund—no hassle 🙂
